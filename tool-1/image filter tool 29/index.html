About Image Filters

Image filters transform the appearance of your photos by applying various visual effects. Adjust the parameters to achieve different moods and styles:

  • Brightness: Controls overall lightness
  • Contrast: Adjusts difference between light and dark areas
  • Saturation: Changes color intensity
  • Hue Rotate: Shifts all colors in the image
  • Blur: Softens the image details
